Flowstep measures your usage in messages. Each plan includes a set number of messages per day and per month. Understanding how messages are counted — and when your limits reset — helps you get the most out of your plan.

## What is a message?

Every time you send a prompt to Flowstep, that counts as one message. This includes initial design requests and follow-up prompts to refine or iterate on an existing design.

<Note>
  If your request results in an error, that attempt does **not** count toward your message limit. You will not lose a message due to a failed generation.
</Note>

## Usage limits

Depending on your plan, you have both a **daily message limit** and a **monthly message limit**. Once you reach either limit, you'll need to wait for it to reset before sending more prompts.

## When limits reset

Your limits reset on a fixed schedule:

- **Monthly limits** reset on the **1st of each month**, regardless of when you signed up or last used Flowstep.
- **Daily limits** reset at **00:00 UTC** every day.

If you're unsure what time 00:00 UTC corresponds to in your timezone, you can use any timezone converter to find the equivalent local time.

## Staying within your limits

If you consistently reach your daily or monthly limits, consider upgrading to a higher plan. Visit the [Pricing](/pricing) page or the [Flowstep website](https://flowstep.ai) to compare plan options.
