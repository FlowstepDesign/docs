---
title: "Get started with Flowstep: sign up and generate designs"
sidebarTitle: "Quick Start"
description: "Create a free Flowstep account, generate your first UI design from a text prompt, and share it with your team — all in one sitting."
---

Getting up and running with Flowstep takes only a few minutes. You sign up for free, describe what you want to build, and Flowstep generates your design immediately. From there you can refine it through follow-up prompts, invite your team to collaborate, and share the result with stakeholders — no design tools or prior experience needed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your free account">
    Go to [flowstep.ai](https://flowstep.ai) and sign up. No credit card is required to get started. Once your account is created, you land directly in the Flowstep editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the screen you want">
    Type a prompt in plain English describing the UI you have in mind. Be specific about the purpose of the screen and any key elements it should include.

    A prompt like "Dashboard for a project management app with a task list, progress bars, and a sidebar navigation" gives Flowstep enough context to produce a focused, useful design.

    <Tip>
      You don't need design vocabulary. Focus on what the screen is for and what information it should show — Flowstep handles the layout, typography, spacing, and color choices.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your screens">
    Submit your prompt and Flowstep generates your design in seconds. You can generate multiple screens in a single session to explore different directions or build out a full user flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine through conversation">
    Not quite right? Type a follow-up prompt to adjust the design — change the color scheme, rearrange elements, swap out content, or request a different layout entirely. You can also edit elements manually without leaving the browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or share your work">
    When you're happy with the result, choose how to share it:

    - **Download** the screen as a JPG or PNG file
    - **Copy to Figma** using the keyboard shortcut to paste directly into an existing Figma file
    - **Share a link** with teammates or stakeholders — they can view the design without creating an account

    <Note>
      You can invite your team to collaborate on a Flowstep file. They can join and start contributing within minutes.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
