Flowstep is an AI-powered design tool that generates production-ready UI screens from plain text prompts. Describe what you want to build, and Flowstep produces high-quality designs instantly. You can refine them through follow-up prompts, export to standard formats, and share with your team — all without leaving the browser.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create a free Flowstep account at [flowstep.ai](https://flowstep.ai). No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your design">
    Type a prompt describing the screen or user flow you want — for example, "Onboarding screen for a fitness tracking app."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and refine">
    Flowstep produces your design instantly. Request variations, edit via chat, or tweak manually until it looks right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or share">
    Export your screens as JPG or PNG, copy them into Figma, or share a link with stakeholders for feedback.
  </Step>
</Steps>
