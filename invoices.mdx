If you're on a paid Flowstep plan, you receive an invoice automatically after every billing cycle. Invoices are generated and stored through Stripe, and you can download them at any time directly from your account dashboard.

## How to find your invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your account dashboard">
    Sign in to Flowstep and navigate to your account settings. Look for the **Billing** section in the sidebar or account menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the billing section">
    In the **Billing** section, locate the invoice history. Flowstep uses Stripe to manage payments, so your invoices are displayed through the Stripe billing portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download an invoice">
    Find the billing cycle you need and click the download link next to it. Your invoice will download as a PDF.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who receives invoices

All paid plan subscribers receive invoices automatically. You do not need to request them manually — Flowstep generates and stores each invoice as soon as a payment is processed.

Free plan users do not receive invoices, as there are no charges on the free tier.

## Questions about a charge

If you see a charge you don't recognize or have a question about an invoice, check your billing cycle dates in the dashboard. For further help, refer to the [Billing](/billing) page or contact Flowstep support.
