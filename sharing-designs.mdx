Flowstep makes it easy to share your designs with anyone who needs to see them. You can generate a shareable link for any design and send it directly to stakeholders—no account setup or software installation required on their end. This removes friction from the feedback process whether you are presenting to executives, clients, or cross-functional teammates.

## Generate a shareable link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the design you want to share">
    Navigate to the design in your Flowstep project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a shareable link">
    Use the share option to create a link for the design. Flowstep generates a unique URL that you can copy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the link">
    Paste the link into an email, Slack message, Notion doc, or wherever your stakeholders will see it. Anyone with the link can open the design immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Viewers do not need a Flowstep account to open a shared design link. They can view the design directly in their browser, making it easy to collect feedback from people outside your team—such as executives, clients, or external partners—without asking them to sign up for anything.
</Note>

## Collaborating with your team

You can add unlimited collaborators to your Flowstep workspace for free. Collaborators can view and work on designs alongside you, making Flowstep practical for teams of any size without unexpected costs.

## When to use shareable links

Shareable links are useful any time you need input or sign-off from people who are not part of your day-to-day design workflow:

- **Executive or leadership reviews** — share early-stage concepts for direction before investing more time.
- **Client presentations** — let clients view designs at their own pace and come back with consolidated feedback.
- **Cross-team alignment** — share flows with engineering, product, or marketing without requiring tool access.
- **Async feedback** — send links to stakeholders in different time zones so they can review when it suits them.
