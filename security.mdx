Flowstep takes security seriously. Every piece of data you send to or store in Flowstep is protected using industry-standard encryption — both while it travels across the network and while it sits on our servers. You can use Flowstep knowing that your designs and project data are handled with care.

## Encryption in transit

All communication between your browser (or any Flowstep client) and Flowstep's servers takes place over HTTPS. This means your data is encrypted the moment it leaves your device and remains encrypted until it reaches our servers. No one intercepting traffic in between can read it.

## Encryption at rest

Your data is encrypted when stored on Flowstep's servers. This protects your information even in the unlikely event that storage media were ever accessed outside of normal operations.

## Your Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy contains the full, authoritative details of how Flowstep collects, stores, and protects your data. If you have questions that aren't answered there, you can reach the Flowstep team directly.

  For complete details on how Flowstep handles your data, refer to the Flowstep Privacy Policy available at [flowstep.ai](https://flowstep.ai). You can also contact the team at [hello@flowstep.ai](mailto:hello@flowstep.ai) with any security-related questions.
</Note>
