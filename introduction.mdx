Flowstep is an AI design tool that turns plain-text descriptions into polished UI screens instantly. Whether you're a product manager sketching out a feature, a founder validating an idea, or a designer exploring concepts quickly, Flowstep removes the friction between thinking about an interface and seeing it — no design background or dedicated software required.

## What you can do with Flowstep

Flowstep covers the full design-to-handoff workflow from a single browser tab:

- **Generate screens from text** — describe what you want and get high-quality UI designs in seconds
- **Edit through conversation** — refine layouts, colors, and content by typing follow-up prompts or adjusting manually
- **Export your work** — download screens as JPG or PNG files ready for presentations or development handoff
- **Share with stakeholders** — send a link to anyone for review; they don't need a Flowstep account to view it
- **Copy directly into Figma** — paste generated screens into an existing Figma file with a single keyboard shortcut

## Who Flowstep is for

Flowstep is built for anyone who needs to visualize a product idea fast:

- **Product managers** who want to communicate UI concepts to engineers without waiting for design resources
- **Founders and entrepreneurs** who need to show investors or users what they're building
- **Designers** who want to explore directions quickly before committing to detailed work
- **Anyone with an idea** who has never used a design tool before

<Note>
  You don't need any design experience to use Flowstep. If you can describe what you want in plain English, Flowstep can generate it.
</Note>
